About Us



OUR MISSION

The Pacific Asian Society of Engineers is a non-profit organization. Our purpose is to promote the development and awareness of the Asian Cultures, provide academic support to members and other underrepresented students, and to help increase preparation in the field of engineering. Events shall be academic, cultural, and social and/or community service in nature. Events are provided to uplift and increase the wellbeing of the students in the College of Engineering at San Diego State University.
